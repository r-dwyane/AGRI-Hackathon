Agri: Nurturing Growth, Harvesting Prosperity - Empowering Farmers for a Sustainable Future.

Agri is a crop management tool, empowers farmers to monitor their crops
through a user-friendly dashboard.




It notifies users when to water plants, provides weather forecasts, and
tracks crop planting and harvesting.




Key benefits of using Agri include real-time insights, enhanced productivity
and efficiency, promotion of sustainable agricultural practices, and support for
data-driven decision-making.







Agri offers farmers a user-friendly dashboard for effective crop management, providing real-time insights on health, pest infestations, and growth progress, with proactive notifications based on data and weather forecasts.
